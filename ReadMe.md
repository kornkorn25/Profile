# 💫 About Me:
I'm a first-year AI and Computer Engineering student at CMKL University (Carnegie Mellon × KMITL), passionate about building technology that solves real-world problems — from predictive systems to AI-powered applications.<br>My experience spans full-stack development, backend API design, and machine learning integration. I've had the opportunity to work with real industry stakeholders, including developing MunchBox, an end-to-end predictive inventory optimization system for Betagro, where I architected the backend, designed the MySQL schema, and built a Raspberry Pi module for automated data ingestion.<br>Beyond software, I bring a competitive engineering mindset — having led the programming team to a Gold Medal at the National Robotics Competition, where I developed real-time control algorithms and decision-flow logic under pressure.<br>I'm driven by the intersection of AI and practical engineering, and I'm always looking for opportunities to contribute to meaningful projects, collaborate with forward-thinking teams, and continue growing as a builder.<br>📫 Open to internships, collaborations, and conversations in the AI & tech space.
